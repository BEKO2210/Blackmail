How does AEGIS work?

1. Setup: Upload your evidence, choose a passphrase and a duress passphrase. Everything is encrypted in your browser.

2. Distribute: The decryption key is split into fragments. Each guardian receives a kit with their fragment and instructions.

3. Check-in: Check in regularly with your passphrase. Your guardians receive a token proving everything is fine.

4. Protection: Being coerced? Use the duress code — the token looks normal, but your guardians detect the hidden signal.

5. Escalation: If check-ins stop, your guardians know what to do. Their fragments together reveal the key.
